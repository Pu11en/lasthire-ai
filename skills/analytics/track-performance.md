# Track Performance Skill

Monitor and analyze your Pinterest affiliate performance.

## Metrics That Matter

| Metric | Why It Matters |
|--------|---------------|
| **Clicks** | Direct traffic to your links |
| **Saves** | Intent to buy later |
| **Repins** | Organic reach |
| **Earnings** | Actual money |
| **CTR** | Click-through rate |

## Commands

### Weekly Report
Tell me to generate your weekly performance report.

Example: "How did my pins do this week?"

### Identify Winners
Find your top-performing pins.

Example: "Which pins are converting best?"

### Optimize
Get recommendations based on data.

Example: "How can I improve my strategy?"

## What To Track

1. **Pin Level**
   - Saves, clicks, repins
   - Best time to pin
   - Board performance

2. **Board Level**
   - Total saves per board
   - Top pins in board
   - Growth rate

3. **Earnings**
   - By product
   - By affiliate program
   - By category

## Action Items

Based on data, always:
- Double down on winners
- Kill what doesn't work
- Test new variations
- Scale what converts
