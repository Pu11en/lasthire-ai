# Generate Pin Ideas Skill

Brainstorm and create Pinterest pin concepts for home decor.

## Pin Formulas That Work

### 1. Product Showcase
"[Product Name] Under $[Price]"
Example: "Velvet Sofa Under $800"

### 2. Room Transformation
"[Room Type] Makeover on a Budget"
Example: "Living Room Makeover Under $500"

### 3. Problem/Solution
"[Problem]? Here's What Works"
Example: "Small Bedroom? Here's What Actually Works"

### 4. Listicle
"[Number] [Item Type] That Transform Your [Room]"
Example: "10 Rugs That Transform Your Living Room"

### 5. Before/After
"[Room Type] Before and After"
Example: "Kitchen Before and After"

## Keywords That Convert

- "Under $X" (budget-conscious)
- "DIY" (self-improvement)
- "Minimalist" (aesthetic)
- "On a Budget" (price-sensitive)
- "Ideas" (planning)
- "Makeover" (transformation)
- "For Small Spaces" (problem-solving)

## Commands

### Generate Ideas
Tell me:
- Product or room type
- Number of ideas needed

Example: "Give me 10 pin ideas for area rugs"

### Write Descriptions
Tell me:
- Pin title
- Product details

Example: "Write a description for Modern Velvet Sofa"

## Output

Returns:
- Pin title
- Description
- Keywords to include
- Board suggestions
- Hashtags

## Best Practices

- Always include price in title for high-ticket
- Use emotional words: cozy, dream, transform
- Add "under $X" for budget appeal
- Match search intent exactly
