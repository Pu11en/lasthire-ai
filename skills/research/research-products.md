# Research Products Skill

Find high-ticket home decor products that convert on Pinterest.

## How It Works

1. Search affiliate networks for trending home decor
2. Filter by price ($200+ = real money)
3. Check commission rates (7%+)
4. Verify save counts on Pinterest

## Commands

### Find Products
Tell me:
- Category (living room, bedroom, kitchen, etc.)
- Price range
- Commission minimum

Example: "Find me living room furniture over $300 with at least 8% commission"

### Analyze Trends
Tell me:
- Keywords to research

Example: "What's trending for modern bedroom ideas?"

### Research Competitors
Tell me:
- Pinterest username to analyze

Example: "What is @target pinning?"

## Output

Returns:
- Product name
- Price
- Commission %
- Potential earnings
- Pinterest save count
- Affiliate link

## Notes

- Always prioritize high-ticket items ($300+)
- Check Pinterest saves as a proxy for interest
- Verify affiliate program acceptance before promoting
