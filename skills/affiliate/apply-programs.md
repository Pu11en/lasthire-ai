## Commands

### List Programs
Show all available programs with details.

Example: "What affiliate programs can I join?"

- Program name

### Track Applications
Keep track of your applications.

Example: "What have I applied to?"

## Application Tips

**For all programs:**
- Say you're a Pinterest content creator
- Focus on home decor niche
- List your Pinterest profile
- Mention expected traffic

**For Amazon:**
- Very easy to get approved
- Just need a website or social account

**For Wayfair:**
- May take 1-2 weeks
- Higher commissions = stricter review
